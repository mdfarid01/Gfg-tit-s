About Our Chapter

Who we are and what we stand for.

Our Mission & Vision

Our primary mission is to create a dynamic and inclusive coding culture at TIT&S. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical industry skills. We envision a community where every student has the opportunity to learn, collaborate on projects, and grow into a competent and confident technologist.

We are committed to providing resources, mentorship, and a platform for students to showcase their talents and prepare for successful careers.

What We Do

Workshops & Seminars

We conduct hands-on workshops on trending technologies like Web Development, AI/ML, and Blockchain.

Coding Contests

Regular competitive programming contests and hackathons to sharpen problem-solving skills.

Project Mentorship

Seniors and mentors guide students in building real-world projects for their portfolios.

Meet the Driving Force

Our chapter is run by a dedicated team of students who are passionate about technology and community building.
